Email, SMS and automation for marketers and agencies

  • Email

Create and send beautiful emails

Sprout helps you create marketing, transactional and automated emails via its advanced drag-and-drop email editor. If you’re looking for creative inspiration you can select from a library of pre-designed templates. You can even link campaigns to the real-world with POS-integrated vouchers and other embeddable media – giving you measurable campaigns on any device.

  • SMS

Send personalised text messages

With a 98% open rate, SMS is a great way to communicate in bulk. Sprout makes it easy to send personalised thank-you messages, staff communications, rostering, alerts and more.

  • Automation

Automate & personalise customer journeys

Sprout lets you engage with customers based on their lifecycle with your brand. Last visit, spend, lack-of spend, milestone anniversaries, birthdays and more can be tracked. Integrated with the systems you already use, an automated journey mapping lets you talk to your customers on a 1-to-1 basis.
